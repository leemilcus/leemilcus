About Me

Music Producer | Singer/Songwritter

Decent Andrew Moyana is a Zimbabwean artist/producer. He is better known by his stage name, Lee Milcus. He possesses a unique blend and passion for Afrobeats, Dancehall, Hip Hop and Gospel. He has progressed from a young boy who fell in love with music at an early age and released his first song in 2016, and has since progressed to become an international star, having worked with individuals from Jamaica, Canada, USA, Ghana and many other countries.
